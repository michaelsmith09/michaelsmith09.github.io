about

Hi, I'm Mike. I'm a former electrician turned software engineer based in northern Utah.


After working electrical on too many slippery rooftops in the icy winter, I decided I wanted a career that didn't risk breaking my neck from thirty foot ladders…. balanced on one foot… while hanging a forty pound chandelier. (True story!)


My favorite thing about running projects as an electrician was making sure the work flowed properly: having the correct supplies delivered on time, sending the right amount of electricians with specific goals for the day, communicating with owners so they were happy each step of the way.


Making things flow well is what I also enjoy doing as a software developer. To transform a vision into a fully functional, tasteful application takes careful planning and, often, creative problem-solving. I enjoy bringing websites to life and building applications that make life easier for others and myself.


When I'm not coding at the computer, you can find me chasing my 3 littles, watching competition shows with my wife, or playing Call of Duty with the guys.
